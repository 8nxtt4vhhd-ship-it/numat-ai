# Numat AI Sales Assistant

## What It Does Today

The app helps sales reps quickly identify which customers may need follow-up and gives useful context before outreach.

It currently provides:

- a live list of customers needing attention
- a territory-based action plan for East and West
- order history and customer summaries
- recent CRM activity and contact history
- visibility of customers already contacted recently

## What Questions It Helps Answer

- Which customers should I look at first?
- Which customers are beyond their usual order cycle?
- Has anyone already contacted this customer recently?
- What has this customer ordered recently?
- What context should I review before I call, email, or visit?

## What Reps Can Already Do In The App

- review the daily action plan
- browse customers needing attention
- view customer order history
- review CRM communication history
- check which accounts may already be in active discussion

## Why This Matters

The goal is not to replace rep judgement.

The goal is to reduce time spent digging through data and help reps focus on:

- the right customers
- the right timing
- the right context

## Potential Next Features

Based on the current app, possible next steps include:

- AI-assisted follow-up drafting
- suggested next action for each customer
- customer visit planning and route planning
- account summaries before outreach
- rep task workflow support
- saving rep activity back into FileMaker

# Numat AI Sales Assistant

## Purpose

The app is designed to help sales reps quickly see which customers may need follow-up, review recent order and contact history, and prioritize outreach based on real customer activity.

It is not intended to replace rep judgement. The aim is to surface the right information at the right time so reps can make faster, better-informed decisions.

## What The App Does Today

### 1. Customer attention tracking

The app reviews order history and identifies customers who may need attention based on their usual ordering cycle.

It currently shows:

- average order cycle
- days since last order
- priority score
- suggested action level

This creates a live view of customers who may be overdue or drifting beyond their normal pattern.

### 2. Today’s Action Plan

The home page provides a territory-based action plan for:

- East
- West

This is designed to give reps a practical working list rather than just charts and reports.

It highlights:

- customers due for attention now
- customers to watch next
- customers already contacted recently, so duplicate follow-up can be avoided

### 3. Order visibility

Reps can browse and filter order data, including:

- customer
- order date
- order value
- order number
- status
- state
- territory
- price list

This gives a fast way to review customer order history and see what has happened recently.

### 4. Customer summary view

Each customer can be viewed as a single summary, including:

- number of orders
- total value
- average value per order
- average order cycle
- last order
- latest activity
- CRM email volume

This gives a quick account-level view before a rep decides whether and how to follow up.

### 5. CRM timeline and contact context

The app now links CRM activity to customers and shows:

- latest CRM activity date
- CRM history count
- recent email timeline
- cleaned previews of communication content

This helps reps understand whether someone has already been in contact and gives context before outreach.

### 6. Live FileMaker integration

The app is now connected to live FileMaker data for:

- orders
- CRM email activity

It also includes a synced CRM cache option so the app can work with larger CRM history without making the site too slow.

## What This Means For Sales Reps

The app already helps answer key questions such as:

- Which customers need attention first?
- Which customers were contacted recently?
- What does this customer normally do?
- What has happened with this customer recently?
- What should I look at before I call, email, or visit?

## Potential Next Features

These are possible next steps based on the data and structure already in place.

### 1. AI outreach drafting

Possible feature:

- generate a suggested follow-up email or call plan from the action list

Possible output:

- recommended next step
- suggested tone
- draft email subject and body
- short call prep notes

Important note:

This would ideally be review-and-edit only at first, not auto-send.

### 2. AI follow-up suggestions

Possible feature:

- explain why a customer is showing as needing attention
- suggest whether to call, email, wait, or plan a visit

This would help reps move from “what is happening?” to “what should I do next?”

### 3. Visit planning / route planning

Possible feature:

- identify customers in the same territory, state, or area who may need attention
- group them into practical visit opportunities
- support rep trip planning and customer visit scheduling

Examples:

- show attention customers near each other
- suggest priority visit routes
- combine overdue customers with customers already in discussion

### 4. AI customer summaries

Possible feature:

- summarize recent order and CRM history into a short briefing

Example output:

- what has changed
- recent contact themes
- account health summary
- suggested talking points before outreach

### 5. Rep workflow support

Possible feature:

- mark accounts as reviewed
- mark follow-up planned
- create a rep task list for the day or week
- track whether a customer is already being handled

### 6. CRM write-back

Possible feature:

- save rep activity or AI-assisted drafts back into FileMaker

This would keep the CRM history complete and allow future AI suggestions to use the most recent communication context.

## Suggested Questions For Sales Reps

To help shape the next phase, useful questions might be:

1. What helps you decide whether to call, email, visit, or wait?
2. What information do you wish you had in front of you before contacting a customer?
3. What would make the action plan more useful day to day?
4. Would drafted outreach save time, or would visit planning be more valuable first?
5. What would make this feel genuinely useful rather than just another report?

## Suggested Message For The Meeting

The app is already able to highlight customers needing attention, show recent order and CRM history, and support daily rep prioritization.

The next phase is not just “adding AI”. The next phase is deciding where AI or automation would be genuinely helpful to the sales team:

- outreach support
- visit planning
- account summaries
- task prioritization

The goal is to build the next features around how reps actually work.
